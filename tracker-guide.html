Credit Card Payoff Tracker — Quick Input Guide

1. Starting Balance

Enter the Total Amount Due from your latest credit card statement. Do not manually edit the balance after this.

2. Monthly Interest Rate

Input the monthly interest rate stated by your bank (example: 3%). Use monthly, not annual, rates.

3. Payment Amount

Enter the amount you plan to pay for the month. This should be at least the minimum amount due.

4. Payment Date

Use a consistent payment date every month. Earlier payments reduce interest and improve accuracy.

5. Expenses (If Included)

Input only fixed monthly obligations. Do not include one-time expenses or charges already included in your balance.

Common Errors to Avoid

  • Manually editing the total balance
  • Entering payments twice
  • Changing payment dates every month
  • Mixing statement balance with current balance

Best Practice: Update the tracker once per billing cycle and let it handle all calculations automatically.
